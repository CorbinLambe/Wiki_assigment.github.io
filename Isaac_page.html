Some Information On Waffles

A Breif Hystory

The origin of waffles starts with flatbread. In midevil times, a mixture made of wheat, water, milk & eggs, known as obelios would be roasted between 2 metal plates over an open flame. These would be known a wafer, while the plates (or irons) would be made with diffrent desighns while being attached to a long handle.

Waffle types

Currently these are all the waffle types that i know of, variations such as; a chocolate chip Waffles do not count. Eggo, Belgian, Leige, Stroop, pancake, Obelio, pastry one, Egg, Waffle-Dogs, Sphere, Speacalty (aka the ones in desired shapes), Waffers, Cracker, Cerial, Stuffed, Toob, Cone, Bowl, Stick

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups of flour
  2. 1 teaspoon of salt
  3. 4 teaspoon of baking powder
  4. 2 tablespoon of sugar
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 1 & half cup of milk
  7. 1/3 cup of melted butter
  8. 1 teaspoon of vanilla ectract

How to Cook

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Beat the eggs, then add the milk, butter, and vanilla
  4. Pour the milk mix into the dry mix, and beat until blended
  5. Pour into waffle iron
  6. Wait for waffle to become gold-ish in color
  7. Remove & eat
