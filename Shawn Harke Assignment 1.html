Why Should you Watch Supernatural?

Watching TV on the weekend

Watching television on the weekends is a great way to spend your time. Being able to sit back and relax while you watch an entertaining show if a fun activity to occupy your time. Supernatural would be a good show to watch during the weekends because it has over 300 episodes for you to immerse yourself in. Each episode is about 45 minutes long with an average of 20 episodes per season. This gives you plenty of content to enjoy during your time off. You can also enjoy the great community that the show has built and delve into the world of interviews and conventions with the cast, as well as several books based on the show.

Meet The Cast

Supernatural contains a wide cast of characters. The two main characters, Sam and Dean Winchester, are played by Jared Padalecki and Jensen Ackles respectfully. The other main actors playing in Supernatural are Misha Collins as Castiel and Alexander Calvert as Jack Kline. Also playing in the show are Jeffery Dean Morgan, Jim Beaver, Mark Sheppard, Kim Rhodes, Mark Pellegrino, Felecia Day, Katie Kassidy, Lauren Cohan, Genevieve Padalecki and many more.
